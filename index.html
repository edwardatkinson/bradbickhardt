“...a healthy and soaring tenor voice.”

Praised for his "healthy and soaring tenor voice" (The Herald Times), as well as his "brilliant physical grace, timing, and precision" (Broadway World), Korean-American tenor Brad Bickhardt is vibrant and versatile in both opera and musical theatre. Mr. Bickhardt has previously been engaged with Opera Theatre of St. Louis, Arizona Opera, Opera Saratoga, Wolf Trap Opera, and The Phoenix Symphony. He is an alumnus of Indiana University and is represented by Stratagem Artists.
